About Us

Who We Are

SCC is a software, cloud and consulting company located in Blumenau, Santa Catarina, the 'Silicon Valley' of Brazil. Founded by Wolfgang Unger with experience in Software Development, Architecture and Consulting of about 20 years in Munich Germany in projects for big enterprises and medium sized companies

What We Do

We are focused on 3 major areas, software development, cloud architecture & engineering and consulting & training. Agile software development, full stack from frontend to backend. Offshore Development for german and european customers and also for local customers in Brazil. Cloud technologies, focused on Amazon Web Services but also Azure and more, for your transition into the cloud. Consulting and Coaching in all IT areas to support your digital business transformation

Why Choose Us

Based on an outstanding experience in big software projects for enterprise as well as small companies, we guarantee high professional solutions for your company. We are the perfect shoring partner for german and european customers as well as your local IT partner in Santa Catarina and Brazil.

Cloud


Cloud Computing

Depending on each customer, there are different reasons and goals for moving into the cloud. With hundreds of cloud providers, offers and technologies it's hard and inextricable for you to select the right one for your business. SCC is your reliable partner and advisor for your transition in the cloud. We have the competence to implement the process and reduce the complexity for your business.

AWS Architecture and Engineering

Our strong focus on cloud technologies is Amazon Web Services. Many years of experience in AWS on many services like Cloud Formation, EC2, S3, ECS, EKS, API Gateway, Code-Pipeline and Code-Build, Cloud Watch and more. Whatever your requirement for AWS is: Storing data on S3, running a application or web-server or wordpress-server on an EC2-instance, setting up a whole development infrastructure with code-pipeline, secure and optimize your web-sites with API-Gateway and Cloudfront, running microservices or applications on ECS, Openshift or Kubernetes. We have the experience to design, consult and implement these tasks.
